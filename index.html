Our Story

A Family Tradition
of Flavour

Zaidi Bakery was born from a grandmother's kitchen in 1995, where every recipe was passed down with love and precision. Today, we blend that heritage with modern techniques to bring you the finest artisan bakes in Islamabad.

Every ingredient is sourced locally. Every product is baked fresh daily. Because at Zaidi, great food starts with great values.
